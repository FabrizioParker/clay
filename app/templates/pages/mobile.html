This is a great new way you can enjoy painting pottery in your HOME • SCHOOL • CHURCH • SYNAGOGUE • OFFICE • COMMUNITY CENTER.
We will bring your choice of preselected pottery or glass fusing, a large selection of colors, and all the necessary tools to make your event a memorable one. We will lay out the colors and materials and give an explanation of the process of the project. When you’re finished, we will label the items and gently pack them up for a safe return back to the studio. Your pieces then get glazed and fired in our kilm.
In about 7 days, we will have the final product ready for you to pick up.
There is a $75 travel fee with a minimum party purchase of $200. (up to 30 miles distance from the studio)
Allow 1 1/2 hours for creating and setting up.

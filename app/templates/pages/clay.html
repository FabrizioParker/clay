Important Information

One Step Further, Or Rather, One Step Back

Many ceramics studios offer only already-fired ware for crafty customers to paint, but our studio takes pottery one step further — or rather, one step back — children ages 6 and older and adults are able to use an electric pottery wheel to shape their own pieces from a lump of clay.

Prior Experience Required

You must have some type of prior experience to use the wheel.If you are familiar with the wheel and would like to just come in and use it (we have three wheels, it is first come first serve unless you make an appointment).

Clay Pricing:

Nine dollars for 1lb of clay; price includes your 1st firing fee. You must buy your clay from Spirit of Clay. No outside clay allowed.

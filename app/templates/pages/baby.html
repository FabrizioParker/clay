It’s Baby Time!

Babies can get involved, too! Your baby’s handprints and/or footprints on a mug, plate, or platter look so great. The paints are lead-free and non-toxic, so they’re safe for your children. First, we paint their hand and press it down on the piece. If it doesn’t turn out the way you want, we can wash it off and start again! At six months, you can do another type of project. Hand and/or foot imprints in clay. This requires a reservation and the pick-up time is longer, but the memory is everlasting.
You may serve beverages, including wine or champagne to add to the ambiance of the day or evening. We will work with your catering service and help with the set-up for your special gathering. Call the studio to make a reservation.

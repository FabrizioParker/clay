Glass Fusing

You can just stop in and we will show you how to work with the glass and basically give you an introductory class, show you how to cut glass and the basics of making a piece of glass fused artwork. You will have the option to pick from many different styles, sizes, and shapes that we offer. The studio is open every day for glass fusing. If you like you may call and make a reservation because glass space is limited. You can make plaques, jewelry, and coasters to name a few projects. Pieces then can be shaped into bowls or plates (slumping) in a second firing. Pieces and prices include materials like hooks, bails. Prices start at $15. Slumping pieces into bowls and/or plates is an additional $5-$10.

 

Steps

1.

You select the glass base.

2.

From our vast selection of glass bits and pieces, you glue down your design.

3.

We will then fuse the piece in our glass kiln.

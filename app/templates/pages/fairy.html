Come build fairy houses out of clay with us for your favorite fairy friend. There are three sizes of fairy houses to choose from, large, medium, and small. The large handbuilt houses are eighty dollars plus tax, the medium handbuilt houses are sixty-five dollars plus tax, and the small handbuilt houses are fourty-eight dollars plus tax. The best thing is that about two weeks or so after making the house you can come back and paint the fairy house to customize it even more. Depending on the season you can make anything from haunted houses to ginger bread houses.

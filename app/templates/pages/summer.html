Summertime… A great time of the year for kids to get a break from school and have fun. Here at Spirit of Clay, we encourage children to tap into their creative side. To step outside their box.
Our summer workshops create a venue for kids to have fun and explore the possibilities of creating without preconceived notions, such as what color a flower should be or whether or not an animal can be painted purple with pink polka dots.
Classes include the pottery wheel, hand-building with clay, painting pottery, glass fusing, and mosaic. Workshop size is anywhere from 1-8 children for most projects and must be paid in full at sign-up. This is refundable if canceled one week prior to the date.
Cost is forty dollars plus tax per day .
You do not have to sign up for the week.
Classes are individual signup
Please call us at 440-684-0001 to sign up!

Spirit of Clay is a dream come true.

How We Got Started

In 2003, Kathy and Kelly took a class at Lakeland Community College called “From Dreams to Reality” and upon completion of the class, decided to become entrepreneurs. In June 2004, after countless hours of research, hiring a consultant, and wise advice from many small business owners, they opened Spirit of Clay.

Changes

In 2015 Kelly decided to leave the studio for other adventures.

Spirit of Clay Now

Kathy continues to operate the studio for it is her passion. Kathy has a strong background in art and appreciates how the creative process can influence one’s life and help one to heal while in the midst of the daily struggles we all experience. She is grateful to have the opportunity to provide this service to the people who walk through her studio doors. Kathy believes in supporting local businesses whenever possible and also offering her services and support to the surrounding communities and the organizations within them.

Safety

Child Safe

The glazes we use are nontoxic and lead free, so children and babies can play without stress or worry. All of the paints we use are washable and will easily come out of your clothes.

Glass Safety Protocol

We have gloves to use for cutting glass so you cannot get hurt by the sharp edges of the glass.

Dishwasher safe

All of our pieces are dishwasher safe but they have to be put on the top self of the dishwasher.

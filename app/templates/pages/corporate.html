Corporate Events

A Spirit of Clay Party

A Spirit of Clay Party is very different from the same old office party! Great for companies Large or Small • Law Firms • Non-Profit Organizations. With a warm atmosphere and great background music, each person can be creative regardless of artistic skill! The Wall Street Journal reported that such parties at contemporary ceramic studios are big hits as they encourage staff and department interaction within a relaxing atmosphere. Companies are always searching for something new to do for parties, and a ceramic studio fits the bill! One of the most popular items to paint are mugs (now everyone will know who left their dirty cup in the sink!) Spirit of Clay offers hundreds of items to choose from. For your party, we can offer you a single per person price for the event or people can select their own pottery at various price points.

Rent the Studio

You may reserve the entire studio for your very own private party for groups such as non-profits, law firms, government organizations, private businesses, and individuals looking for a private space to create and have fun. You can bring food and beverages, including beer & wine. Whether it’s an employee/family event or staff appreciation day, we will give your group an enjoyable, creative experience. We require a 3-week advance notice to guarantee space availability. Reservation Fee: $25 (This fee must be paid at the time of reservation and will be applied to the cost of the party; this deposit is nonrefundable.
Studio Private Rental: $250.00 // Cost of pottery and studio fee not included in party rental fee. You have 2 hours, more time is allotted for a pro-rated fee. Rentals on the weekend need to start at 9 am OR the last two hours of opening. Rentals during the week can be more flexible at times.

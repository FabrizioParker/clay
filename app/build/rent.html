Rent the Studio

You may reserve the entire studio for your very own private party for groups such as non-profits, law firms, government organizations, private businesses, and individuals looking for a private space to create and have fun. You can bring food and beverages, including beer & wine. Whether it’s an employee/family event or staff appreciation day, we will give your group an enjoyable, creative experience.
We require a 3-week advance notice to guarantee space availability.
Studio Private Rental: $250.00 // Cost of pottery not included in party rental fee.
You have 2 hours, more time is allotted for a pro-rated fee.
Rentals on the weekend need to start at 9 am OR the last two hours of opening.
Rentals during the week can be more flexible at times, please inquire.

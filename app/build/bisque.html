Get Started
 

We Provide Everything You Need…Easy as 1, 2, 3!

1. You pick your piece.

We have a very large selection of items to choose from plates, platters, vases, trivets, wine glass stems, picture frames, teapots, mugs, animal figurines, dog bowls, banks, and much, much more.

2. You paint and decorate your masterpiece.

Spirit of Clay offers over 80 colors to choose from, as well as a variety of stamps, stencils, and books to help you with your designing. we are always more than happy to help and assist you in any way.

3. We glaze and fire your masterpiece within 5-7 days.

When you complete your masterpiece, we apply a clear glaze and fire the piece in our kilns. Once the piece is fired, it is dinnerware safe. Your piece is then ready to be picked up.

Please Note:

No added studio fee! Spirit of Clay will not hold finished or unfinished pieces for more than 45 days unless prior arrangements are made. Cost is easy! All of the bisque is marked with the price. Included in the cost of the piece are all of your paints and your firing. We are all-inclusive! We have a large selection of stencils, stamps, colorful idea books, and displays. We will also match the price of any of our competitor's pieces if you show us proof.
